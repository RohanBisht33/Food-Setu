How It Works

A simple, 3-step process to bridge the gap between waste and want.

1. Post Surplus Food

Restaurants & stores instantly post details of their surplus food, including type, quantity, and a pickup window before it expires.

2. Get Notified

Our system sends real-time, geo-targeted alerts to nearby shelters, NGOs, and individuals who can make use of the food.

3. Claim & Collect

Recipients claim the food, confirming the pickup. This prevents duplication and ensures the food reaches those in need efficiently.
